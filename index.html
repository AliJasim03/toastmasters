About The DTAC 2025 Conference

The DTAC 2025 Conference is a celebration of public speaking excellence, where participants showcase their oratory skills and leadership potential. The event brings together passionate speakers, mentors, and audiences, fostering a community of growth and inspiration.

Where

Kingdom of Bahrain

When
